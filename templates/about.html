About Us

CodeWizardsHQ gives students the necessary skills to build platforms like this one. Over the course of several years of study in live, online coding classes, students acquired the necessary skills to develop and deploy a full-stack web application that helps users solve real-world problems.

As a culmination of all their hard work at CodeWizardsHQ, we offer students the chance to gain real-world experience by working on coding projects for nonprofits, where our students apply for and are selected to participate in projects over 12 weeks. This project was made by David, Aditya, Daniel, Adam, Seojin, Saketh, and our amazing instructor Peter Ferri!
